What is RHS Robotics?

RHS Robotics is a club of our schools SkillsUSA chapter that focuses on growth and development in the engineering and robotics fields. Students at Rochester are encouraged to take the provided robotics and engineering classes not just for CTE credits but to learn more about the field. 

What do we offer?

RHS robotics offer the Robotics: Urban, Search and Rescue and sUAS commercial drone competitions at Regional, State, and National level.
